About Bethany

Learn more

Bethany has worked on projects for noted authors Randy Alcorn, Henry Blackaby, Lisa Whelchel, and Tony Evans. She’s also a writer of Christian curriculum, faith-building articles, and leader guides. Her work has been featured in Answers magazine, in Jennifer Rothschild's Missing Pieces: Real Hope When Life Doesn't Make Sense, and in Beth Moore’s James: Mercy Triumphs.

Whether helping authors perfect their messages of faith prior to print or condensing inspirational trade books into workbook format, Bethany uses her eye for detail to strengthen Christian discipleship efforts. Helping people to clearly and enthusiastically convey the difference Jesus Christ makes in lives is central to her work.

Should you become a client of Faith-Based Editorial Services, you can expect encouragement and idea prompts meant to stimulate professional and spiritual growth. Bethany begins with a critique of your work as a whole. Is the message solid? Does it convey what you intend? Might your project find a place in today's competitive Christian market? The subsequent editing process is a learning experience that both polishes the message and strengthens the writer. Even recognized authors benefit from the honest feedback of an experienced editor invested in the gospel mission.
